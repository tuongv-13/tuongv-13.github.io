My process for this zine involved:

This zine called sincerely, yours was made using screenprinting techniques, as well as bookbinding. It is a token of my love and dedication to my family.

  1. Collecting photographs of my family
  2. Digitizing photos using the Adobe Suite
  3. Preparing the materials for the printmaking process:
    1. ↳ Prepping silkscreens
    2. ↳ Burning images onto screens
    3. ↳ Have fun pulling prints!
  4. Binding the zine
  5. Sharing with family & friends!
